What is Indignation?

Indignation is an Action RPG currently being developed in Unity
It is a game designed around a single boss fight.
With mechanics centered around indepth 3D combat and managing resources.

Goals

To create a proof of concept for future projects
To create responsive Artifical Intelligence within a 3D environment
To create challenging and cinematic combat
